Work Process:

  • I recorded a video using my phone.
  • I uploaded the video to my pc.
  • I open the video in BeeCut software.
  • I cut the video to 1 minute long.
  • I added the opening page with the title and my information.
  • I added the subtitles, transactions, credit page and ending page.
  • I added the music to the soundtrack of the video as background music..
